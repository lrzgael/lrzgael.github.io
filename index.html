About me

I am Gaël Le Ruz, a PhD student at the Laboratoire Jacques-Louis Lions and Inria paris, working within the COMMEDIA and ANANKE Inria teams. My research is supervised by Philippe Moireau and Damiano Lombardi.

Research interests

My research focuses on the design of robust data assimilation strategies for constrained physical systems. During my PhD, we developed optimal observer formulations on manifolds, from a deterministic perspective, with applications to front propagation problems.
